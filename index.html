    Publication

    Estimating Air Pollution Levels from Images using a VGG16-based Ensemble:

  • • In this paper we have aimed on finding particle PM 2.5 from an image. Particle 2.5 basically indicates the pollution level. It consists of 1885 photographs with different PM 2.5 levels taken in Shanghai, China at the Oriental Pearl tower.
  • • We have extracted three features named as entropy, contrast and structural information extraction. After extracting these features individually we have trained these model in separate VGG16 transfer learning. These features have acted as base learner.
  • • A meta learner was developed on top of the three homogenous base learners. Multiple machine learning algorithms were tested on top of the base learners. Random forest regressor gave the best performance in terms of predictions.
  • • We were able to achieve RMSE of 13.81 and R2 of 0.884.

    • I have written this paper under Dr. Ravi Shankar Singh(Associate Professor,Department of Computer Science and Engineering, IIT BHU).This paper is under review by SPIE international journal.
